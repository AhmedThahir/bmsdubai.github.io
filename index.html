Aprilia

Aprilia is an Italian motorcycle manufacturer, owned by Piaggio. Aprilia has produced large sportbikes such as the 1,000 cc V-twin RSV Mille and the V4 RSV4.

History

Aprilia was founded immediately after World War II, in Noale, Italy by Alberto Beggio. The company started as a manufacturer of bicycles; it then moved on to manufacture scooters and small-capacity motorcycles. The company was acquired by Piaggio in 2004.
